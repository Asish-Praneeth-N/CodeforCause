Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon grated lemon zest
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tablespoons minced fresh basil, divided
  • 4 tilapia fillets (6 ounces each)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a small bowl, whisk lemon zest, lemon juice, oil and garlic until blended; stir in capers and 2 tablespoons basil.
  2. Step 2: Reserve 2 tablespoons mixture for drizzling cooked fish.
  3. Step 3:Brush remaining mixture onto both sides of tilapia; sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Step 4: Grill tilapia on a lightly oiled rack, covered, over medium heat or broil 4 in. from heat until fish just begins to flake easily with a fork, 3-4 minutes on each side.
  5. Step 5: Drizzle with reserved lemon mixture; sprinkle with remaining basil.
  6. Step 6: Recipe is Ready to serve
