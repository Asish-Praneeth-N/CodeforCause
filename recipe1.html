Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 1 large shallot, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground pepper
  • 2-3 teaspoons white balsamic vinegar
  • ⅓ cup crumbled goat cheese
  • ¼ cup pomegranate seeds

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Step 2: Toss Brussels sprouts with shallot, oil, salt and pepper in a medium bowl. Spread on a large rimmed baking sheet.
  3. Step 3:Roast the Brussels sprouts until tender, 20 to 22 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Return to the bowl and toss with vinegar to taste.
  5. Step 5: Sprinkle with goat cheese and pomegranate seeds.
  6. Step 6: Recipe is Ready to serve
